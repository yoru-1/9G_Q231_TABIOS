DIFFERENT DOG BREEDS



      Chow Chow
      • The Chow Chow has a striking appearance, with deep-set eyes, a large head, and a mane of hair.

      • Typically, Chow Chows are not extroverted, sociable dogs.

      • They can be aggressive with other dogs and have a tendency to be distant around strangers, but they are devoted and protective of their family.

      Siberian Husky
      • The Siberian Husky is medium-sized, with a dense double coat, and erect ears.

      • Siberian huskies are extroverted. They are outgoing and energetic.

      • They were developed to work in packs, pulling light loads at moderate speeds over vast frozen expanses.

      Pomeranian
      • The Pomeranian is a vivacious toy dog breed with a long coat and recognizable neck ruff.

      • Typically, Pomeranians are friendly but can be bossy at times.

      • The Pomeranian can be a wonderful companion with the proper training, but this breed is not for everyone.

      Aspin
      • The Aspin is a medium-sized dog that has a short coat, and is usually short and rough with varying colors.

      • Aspins are very shy, lovable, resilient, and low-maintenance.

      • They are a mixture of various breeds, but it is unclear as to who their true ancestors were.
